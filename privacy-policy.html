Information We Collect


Privacy Policy for Halfway Cafe Effective date: July 18, 2026


Halfway Cafe does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information.
The app:
Does not require users to create an account. Does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information. Does not use analytics or advertising services. Does not store user-entered addresses or location information.
Users may enter addresses, cities, or states to calculate a meeting point. This information is used only during the current session to provide results and is not stored by Halfway Cafe.
Third-Party Services
Halfway Cafe may open Apple Maps or Google Maps when users choose to navigate to a selected coffee shop. The use of those services is subject to their respective privacy policies.
Children's Privacy
Halfway Cafe does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
